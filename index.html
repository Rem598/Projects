Left-Digit Bias Audit: Pricing Psychology

Does X.99 pricing actually work? I analyzed 185,000+ transactions to find out.

Finding: It works for some products, not others. Phones showed +5.16% lift with X.99 pricing (p<0.001). Laptops? No effect. Pricing psychology is category-specific, not universal.

Impact: Clear evidence for when to use charm pricing vs standard pricing. Not all price points are created equal.
